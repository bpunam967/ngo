About Us

Samriddhi is a research based agriculture company dedicated to transfer to typical Nepalese low return subsistence agriculture into thriving agro enterprises through the promotion of high value crops. Nepal's characteristic micro-climates in the hills and mountains are highly favorable for the production of high quality berries,nuts and fruits. Promotion of the high value crops that have comparative advantages for Nepal wil create new business opportunities in various sectors including production,processing and marketing. Samriddhinis integrating demand-driven research,extension and agriculture enterpreneurship in a single functional system to expedite the time lag between innovatios and income generation. Our execution model involves functional collaboration among various agencies including national and international Universities,private companies , farmer's cooperatives and government agencies. We promote private-public-community partnership to deliver research based information and services to our clients. We aim to offer one stop solutions to our clients.
